Chicken curry

Indian curry chicken is a flavorful dish featuring tender chicken cooked in a rich, aromatic sauce made with a blend of spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. The sauce is often creamy, with tomatoes and yogurt adding depth and balance to the heat from chili peppers. It's typically served with rice or naan for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves-cut into bite-size pieces
